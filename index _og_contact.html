Examples

From big studio films to cutting edge technology, our focus is on high stakes partnerships and opportunities.

Feature Films

We've sent feature films to festivals such as Tribeca and TIFF with distribution from companies such as Samuel Goldwyn Films. We’ve worked with amazing partners such as Animal Kingdom, RSA, Dogfish, and Archer Grey.

We’ve contributed to many tv shows, scripted and non-scripted, live action and animation. We brought youtubers to broadcast and the other way around. Our work has lead to broadcast on networks such as Trutv, College Humor, and Comedy Central.
